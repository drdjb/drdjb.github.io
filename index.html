Me, Myself & IT

My research lies in the modelling and simulation of human journeys (movement and decision making) – typically driven by machine learning and big data. New perspectives are often drawn from the synthesis and hybridization of models from data (software and hardware, humans and services). I collaborate with a range of organisations from SMEs to the NHS, often resulting in innovative mobile or digital artefacts.
